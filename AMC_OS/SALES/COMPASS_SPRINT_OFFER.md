### The 7 Maturity Dimensions

1. **Agent Architecture & Design** — how agents are structured, composed, and orchestrated
2. **Reliability & Failure Handling** — error rates, fallback strategies, recovery patterns
3. **Observability & Monitoring** — logging, tracing, alerting, cost tracking
4. **Governance & Compliance** — policies, access controls, audit trails, human-in-the-loop
5. **Evaluation & Testing** — evals, benchmarks, regression testing, ground truth
6. **Deployment & Operations** — CI/CD, rollback, versioning, scaling
7. **Business Alignment** — ROI tracking, stakeholder reporting, roadmap integration

### Maturity Levels

- **L1 — Ad Hoc:** No formal process; individual heroics
- **L2 — Emerging:** Some practices exist but inconsistent
- **L3 — Defined:** Documented, repeatable processes across agents
- **L4 — Managed:** Metrics-driven with continuous improvement loops
- **L5 — Optimized:** Automated governance, predictive quality, industry-leading
