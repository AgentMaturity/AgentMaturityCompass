### Objection Playbook (Top 5)
1. **"Too expensive"** → "The Sprint replaces 2-3 weeks of internal audit work. What's that costing you in engineer time today?"
2. **"We'll do it internally"** → "Most teams try that first. The gap: no external benchmark and no reusable framework. That's what the Sprint provides."
3. **"Not a priority right now"** → "When is your next board/compliance review? We size this to complete before that deadline."
4. **"Need to involve others"** → "Absolutely. Can we do a 15-min briefing with them this week? I'll prep a 1-pager for the conversation."
5. **"What's the ROI?"** → "Clients use the maturity score to prioritize $50k-$200k agent investments. The Sprint costs 1% of that and prevents the wrong bet."
