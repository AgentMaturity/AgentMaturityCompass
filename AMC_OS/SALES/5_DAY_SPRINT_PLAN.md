# 5-Day Sales Sprint Plan — Close $5,000

**Goal:** Close one AMC Compass Sprint ($5,000) in 5 business days.
**Fallback:** If no close by Day 5, have ≥3 active proposals in pipeline.

## Weekly Retrospective Questions

1. What outreach angle got the best response rate?
2. What objection came up most? Is the battlecard working?
3. What's the average time from first touch to call booked?
4. What's the biggest bottleneck: leads, replies, calls, or closes?
5. What would I do differently next week?

## Emergency Playbook — If Day 3 and Zero Replies

Something is wrong with messaging, targeting, or channel. Debug:

1. **Check deliverability:** Send test emails to a friend. Check spam folders.
2. **Check targeting:** Are you reaching actual decision makers, or gatekeepers?
3. **Check messaging:** Forward your outreach to 3 people you trust. Ask "would you reply to this?" Listen to the honest answer.
4. **Switch channels:** If email isn't working, go all-in on LinkedIn DMs. Or try warm intros exclusively.
5. **Lower the ask:** Instead of selling the Sprint, offer a free 15-min "maturity gut-check." Convert to Sprint on the call.
6. **Post more:** LinkedIn organic reach compounds. Post daily. Comment on 20 posts per day.

**Do NOT:** Lower the price. Spam more volume with bad messaging. Panic.
