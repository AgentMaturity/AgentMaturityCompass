## The Offer
**White-Label Compass Sprint:** Agency brands AMC's 5-day maturity assessment as their own service.
- Agency gets: full rubric, assessment methodology, deliverable templates, POLARIS-backed execution
- Client gets: 7-dimension maturity score, evidence vault, 30/60/90 roadmap
- Agency charges: $5,000–$8,000 per client
- Agency pays AMC: $3,000 per assessment
- Minimum commitment: None (pay per Sprint)
- Scale pricing: 5+ Sprints/quarter → $2,500/Sprint
