Executive Summary

InvoiceBot v2 demonstrates solid operational foundations with strong governance and operating model practices, achieving Level 4.0 in both dimensions. The agent processes approximately 2,400 invoices per day with a 94.2% automation rate, reflecting mature deployment and operational workflows. Security and reliability are well-established at Level 3.5, with appropriate access controls and monitoring in place.

Key gaps exist in evaluation coverage (Level 2.0) and cost efficiency monitoring (Level 2.5). Evaluation is limited to 42 happy-path test cases with no adversarial testing or regression detection. Cost attribution remains at the aggregate level, preventing optimization of routing decisions and per-transaction economics.

Primary recommendation: establish a continuous evaluation pipeline with 200+ test cases and implement per-transaction cost tracking within 30 days. These two interventions address the most significant risk vectors — undetected failure modes and unoptimized spend — and form the foundation for achieving Level 4+ maturity across all dimensions within 90 days.
