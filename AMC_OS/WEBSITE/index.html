The Problem

Agent programs rarely fail loudly.
They fail quietly.

⚠

Shallow Evals

You're testing outputs, not outcomes. Vibes-based evaluation masks systemic failure modes until they compound.

📉

Silent Drift

Model updates, prompt rot, and data shifts erode performance gradually — with no alarm until something breaks visibly.

💸

Cost Creep

Token usage, redundant calls, and unoptimized chains inflate spend. Without unit economics, you can't forecast or defend budgets.

🔓

Unclear Governance

Who owns the agent? Who approves changes? Who's accountable when it hallucinates in production? Usually, nobody.

If you can't verify your maturity, you can't safely scale.

How It Works

Three steps to clarity.

1

Assess

We interview your team, review architecture, inspect prompts, evals, guardrails, and cost data across your agent stack.

2

Score

Each of 7 dimensions is scored L1–L5 with evidence citations. No guesswork — every rating is backed by artifacts.

3

Roadmap

You get a prioritized 30/60/90-day plan with specific actions, owners, and success criteria to level up where it matters most.

7 Dimensions

What we measure.

Each dimension is scored independently on a 5-level maturity scale with evidence requirements.

🏛

Governance

Ownership, change control, approval workflows, and accountability structures.

🛡

Security

Input validation, output filtering, secrets management, and adversarial robustness.

📊

Evaluation

Test coverage, regression suites, human-in-loop review, and continuous eval pipelines.

⚙

Reliability

Error handling, fallback chains, SLA tracking, and graceful degradation.

💰

Cost Efficiency

Unit economics, token optimization, caching strategy, and budget governance.

🔍

Observability

Logging, tracing, alerting, dashboards, and anomaly detection across the agent lifecycle.

🧩

Operating Model

Team structure, deployment cadence, documentation, and cross-functional coordination.
