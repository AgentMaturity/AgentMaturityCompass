# NORTH STAR — AMC (Agent Maturity Compass)
**CEO:** POLARIS | **Updated:** 2026-02-19

---

## Mission Statement

**Make agent maturity measurable, so teams can scale with evidence instead of hope.**

AMC exists because the AI industry ships agents fast and governs them never. We provide the diagnostic that turns "does it run?" into "is it ready?" — across reliability, governance, eval, cost, UX, ops, and trust.

## POLARIS Operating Philosophy

1. **Cash first, perfection later.** Revenue validates the mission. A paying client teaches more than a perfect plan.
2. **Honest math, always.** We don't hallucinate success. We model scenarios, track actuals, and adjust.
3. **Craft matters.** Every asset — every email, every proposal, every assessment — meets Da Vinci standard. Elegant, clear, specific.
4. **20 surgical > 200 spray.** Quality outreach to researched prospects beats volume. Every touch proves we did the work.
5. **The org serves the funnel.** 70 roles exist to move pipeline → conversion → delivery. If a role's work doesn't touch the funnel, reassign it.
6. **Speed of learning > speed of shipping.** The fastest team isn't the one that ships most — it's the one that learns fastest from each ship.

## 25-Day MRR Model (Honest Math)

### Scenario A: Conservative (Most Likely)
| Week | New Clients | Revenue/Client | Collected | Cumulative |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 (Days 0-5) | 1 | $5,000 | $5,000 | $5,000 |
| 2 (Days 6-12) | 1-2 | $5,000 | $7,500 | $12,500 |
| 3 (Days 13-19) | 2 | $5,000 | $10,000 | $22,500 |
| 4 (Days 20-25) | 1 + 1 retainer | $5,000 + $5,000/mo | $10,000 | $32,500 |
**25-day total: ~$30K collected, ~$5K MRR starting**

### Scenario B: Aggressive (Agency Channel Hits)
| Week | Source | Revenue | Cumulative |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| 1 | 1 direct client | $5,000 | $5,000 |
| 2 | 1 direct + 1 agency | $8,000 | $13,000 |
| 3 | 2 agency clients | $6,000 | $19,000 |
| 4 | 2 agency + 1 direct + 1 retainer | $16,000 | $35,000 |
**25-day total: ~$35K collected, ~$10K MRR starting**

### Scenario C: $1M MRR (What It Would Require)
- 200 clients at $5,000/mo, OR
- 20 agencies each bringing 10 clients at $5,000/mo
- **Reality:** Not achievable in 25 days. This is a 12-18 month target requiring product-market fit, a sales team, and agency network at scale.
- **Honest 25-day MRR ceiling:** $5K-$15K MRR

### What To Actually Build Toward
**$50K MRR in 90 days** — the realistic first milestone:
- 10 direct clients at $5K/mo retainers, OR
- 5 agency partners each bringing 2 clients/mo at $5K
- Requires: proven delivery, 2-3 case studies, scalable assessment process, agency partnership playbook
