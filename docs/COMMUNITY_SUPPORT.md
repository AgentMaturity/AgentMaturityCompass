# COMMUNITY_SUPPORT.md — Sponsorship, support, and community paths

AMC needs both community energy and commercial clarity.

## There are three different paths

### 1. Community participation
For contributors, users, and ecosystem builders.

See:
- `docs/COMMUNITY.md`
- `CONTRIBUTING.md`

### 2. Sponsorship
For people or companies who want to support the open-source project.

See:
- `SPONSORING.md`

### 3. Product / commercial engagement
For teams that want deployment, support, enterprise packaging, or services.

See:
- `docs/PRICING.md`
- `docs/PRODUCT_EDITIONS.md`
- `docs/DEPLOYMENT_OPTIONS.md`
- `docs/ENTERPRISE.md`

## What this page exists to prevent
A lot of open-source projects blur support, sponsorship, and product pricing into one muddy bucket.
That is lazy and confusing.
AMC should keep them separate.
