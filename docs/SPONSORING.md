# SPONSORING.md — Support AMC as open-source trust infrastructure

AMC is open-source trust infrastructure for AI agents.
If it helps you, you can support the project without confusing sponsorship with product pricing.

## Sponsorship vs pricing

- **Sponsorship** supports the health of the open project.
- **Pricing** pays for product access, support, services, or enterprise deployment value.

If you want to back the mission, sponsor.
If you want product/service value, buy the relevant offering.

## Why sponsor AMC
Sponsorship helps fund:
- maintenance
- docs and examples
- compatibility testing
- assurance packs
- community support
- research-to-product translation

## Suggested sponsor tiers

| Tier | Intended supporter | Public recognition direction |
|---|---|---|
| **Supporter** | individuals | name listed if desired |
| **Backer** | heavy users / small teams | supporter wall acknowledgement |
| **Sustainer** | recurring company support | logo/name acknowledgement |
| **Ecosystem Partner** | integration or ecosystem supporters | partner acknowledgement and ecosystem spotlight |
| **Strategic Sponsor** | major long-term supporters | sponsor callout and roadmap/community briefings |

## Sponsorship principles
- sponsorship does not buy editorial control over trust conclusions
- sponsorship does not compromise benchmark independence
- sponsorship supports the ecosystem, not score manipulation
