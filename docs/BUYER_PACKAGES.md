# BUYER_PACKAGES.md — Commercial offers by buyer type

## Startup / small AI team
Best fit:
- Free or Starter / Pro
- focus on Score, Shield, basic Watch

## Platform / engineering team
Best fit:
- Team
- focus on Score, Shield, Watch, some Fleet, selected Comply

## Regulated organization
Best fit:
- Enterprise / Regulated
- focus on Shield, Enforce, Vault, Watch, Comply

## Enterprise security / governance team
Best fit:
- Enterprise
- focus on Enforce, Vault, Watch, Fleet, Passport, Comply

## OSS maintainer / ecosystem partner
Best fit:
- Free + sponsorship / ecosystem partnership path

## Why this page exists
To answer the buyer question: "what do we buy?" without making them infer it from raw feature lists.
