How It Works

Evidence you can
mathematically verify.

01 — Observe

Capture every decision

AMC sits between your agent and its LLM provider. Every API call, tool use, and decision is captured as a cryptographically signed evidence artifact. Nothing is self-reported.

02 — Score

111 evidence-gated questions

Five dimensions of maturity across six levels (L0–L5). Each answer requires execution proof — you can't claim a level without the evidence to back it up. Anti-gaming protections prevent score inflation.

03 — Prove

Tamper-evident ledger

Every score is backed by a Merkle-tree evidence chain. Tamper with one entry and the entire chain breaks. Export an Agent Passport as portable, verifiable proof of maturity.
